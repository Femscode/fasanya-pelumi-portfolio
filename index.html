CT-HOSTEL

CTHostel is a cutting-edge real estate technology platform focused on simplifying the hostel acquisition process for students in Nigeria's tertiary institutions. It connects students with verified hostel agents and real estate personnel, ensuring a secure and hassle-free experience in finding and renting student accommodations.

  • Agents Onboarding.
  • Hostel Management.
  • Roommate Matching.

CT-TASTE

CTTaste is an online food ordering system tailored for tertiary institutions in Nigeria. It simplifies the process of ordering food from various vendors, offering a user-friendly platform for both customers and vendors.

  • Web Application and RESTful APIs.
  • Payment Integration.
  • Order Management & Sales Report
